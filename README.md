\# Cash Flow Analysis \& Liquidity Forecasting



\## Overview



This project analyzes operating, investing, and financing cash flows to forecast future liquidity and identify potential cash-gap risks.



The objective is to provide visibility into working capital runway and support proactive treasury and liquidity management decisions.



\## Tools Used



\- Python

\- pandas

\- NumPy

\- statsmodels (ARIMA)

\- matplotlib

\- Jupyter Notebook



\## Project Features



\- Net Cash Flow Analysis

\- Ending Cash Balance Tracking

\- Working Capital Monitoring

\- Liquidity Runway Calculation

\- Cash Gap Detection

\- ARIMA-Based Cash Flow Forecasting

\- MAE and MAPE Validation

\- Future Cash Position Forecasting

\- Treasury Analytics Reporting
